SCRAMBLE SQUARE

Tokyu Hands

Located on the 10th floor, Tokyu Hands focuses on hobby, home improvement and lifestyle products. Products include toys, games, novelty items, gift cards, gift wrap, costumes, bicycles, travel products, hobby materials, household hardware, tools, do-it-yourself kits, pet supplies, office supplies and stationery; calligraphy, painting, drawing supplies, furniture, lighting, home appliances, and storage solutions.

Tsutaya & Starbucks

Located on the 11th floor, Tsutaya and Starbucks lets you enjoy your coffee while reading. The wonderful aroma of coffee, the depth of its taste, and the expert knowledge of our friendly baristas are all a part of the refreshing and unique Starbucks Experience, a special experience that no other coffeehouse can offer.

Moheji

Located on the 12th floor, Moheji is a Monjayaki restaurant located on the 12th floor. Monjayaki is a type of Japanese pan-fried batter, popular in the Kantō region, similar to okonomiyaki, but that uses different liquid ingredients. Not only is this dish tasty, it also is fun to make yourself or even watch the waiter make it for you.

PARCO

Pokémon Center

Located on the 6th floor of Shibuya Parco near the Nintendo Tokyo store, Pokémon Center Shibuya features some truly visionary decorations in its entryway. While most Pokémon Centers opt for white walls and colorful starter Pokémon as their mascots to welcome visitors, Shibuya went for a Pokémon a little more fitting its image—Mewtwo.

Nintendo Store

Nintendo Tokyo is located on the 6th floor alongside Pokemon Center Shibuya and other gaming and anime-themed shops. The entire 6th floor of the Parco building has been dubbed “Cyberspace Shibuya” and includes other shops such as the Capcom Store and Jump Shop.

Bait

Established in California, USA in 2012, "BAIT" combines the culture of anime and comics with street culture. They handle a lot of original kids apparel, popular sneakers, apparel and others. They are also developing their own products.

MIYASHITA PARK
KitKat Chocolatory

Located on the 2nd floor, KitKat Chocolatory is a place where you get to create your own custom KitKat and try limited flavors. If you want to get your loved ones chocolate for valentines day, or simply get souvenirs for your friends and family, this may be the place.

The Shibuya Souvenir Store

Located on the 2nd floor, The Shibuya Souvenir Store provides all kinds of unique souvenirs related to Shibuya's culture. If you want to get an unique unusual souvenir for your friends and family, this may be the place.

Rooftop Park

Located on the top floor is a rooftop which has a skatepark, rock climbing area and a beach soccer field. A lot of people come to this rooftop to chill as there are a lot of places to sit and have a conversation with your friend.

HIKARIE
Nurukan Sato

Located on the 7th floor, Murukan Sato is a restaurant notorious for its sake. They serve more than 30 types of sake and also serve a large selection of sparkling sake. If you are new to sake or are weak to alcohol, they are serve low-alcohol easy to drink sake.

Yasaiya Mei

Yasai Mei, located on the 7th floor, is a Japanese cuisine restaurant that serves Shabu Shabu & Sukiyaki. Shabu-shabu is a Japanese hotpot dish of thinly sliced meat and vegetables boiled in water and served with dipping sauces. Sukiyaki consists of meat which is slowly cooked or simmered at the table, alongside vegetables and other ingredients, in a shallow iron pot in a mixture of soy sauce, sugar, and mirin.

Sky Lobby

Located on the 11th floor, Sky Lobby is famous for having great views of the city. We recommend this place as its never crowded and it also is free. Wonderful for a sunny day or at night.

STREAM
Nakayoshi

Located on the 2nd floor, Nakayoshi is a popular and well-loved restaurant that has been serving in Ebisu for 36 years. Enjoy the savory home-style dishes and freshly-cooked rice in earthen pots. Alcoholic beverages are also served in the evenings.

Sushi Tokyo Ten

Located on the 3rd floor, Sushi Tokyo Tens is a fancy omakase style (chef's choice) sushi restaurant. Their concept is to deliver "a little of plenty of the foods in season." to their guests. Please enjoy their selections of Sushi and snacks in an omakase style.

Square

Located right next to the Shibuya Stream building, are two outdoor spaces on the ground floor built through a private-public initiative to revive the Shibuya River. They hold various events at the square and guests will be able to enjoy a pleasant time by the river.

MODI
Maimu

Located on the 4th floor, Maimu is a kimonu rental service mainly for special occasions such as graduation ceremonies. This is a great opportunity for you to not only wear a kimono but also take nice photos with you and your loved ones.

Rainbow Karaoke

Located at the very top of the building is Rainbow Karaoke, a karaoke service. Compared to other karaoke services, Rainbow Karaoke is pop and stylish and has the perfect atmosphere setup for you to have a blast with your friends and family.

Cheese Dish Factory

Located on the 9th floor is a restaurant that loves cheese! If you ever feel a bit home sick and want to eat western food this is the place. The restaurant has nice interior and lighting giving it the perfect atmosphere.

CATSTREET
Tiffany & Co.

Tifanny & Co. is a famous luxury brand mostly known for its jewelry. Althought the prices can be insanely high for most people, it is worth having a look at the store in Cat Street.

Iyoshi Cola is a unique pop cola store. The cola they make are made with real cola nuts based on a recipe that is over 100 years old. It also includes certain spices.
